Crystals Collector!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number to match at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your crystal total.

You win the game by matching your crystal total to a the random number. You lose if your crystal total goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new round starts, the values of each crystal change.
